03/Method

In focus 01 / 05

The system I build.

Same shape every time - small, bounded, and yours to keep.

  1. 01 Find the leak

    I pick one workflow that eats time or keeps getting redone, and find where it actually breaks.

  2. 02 Redesign it

    I map what should happen: the steps, the inputs, what AI handles, what a human decides.

  3. 03 Build it with what you have

    I build it into the tools you already use - no new platform to adopt.

  4. 04 Keep a human in the loop

    AI drafts and prepares; a person approves anything that matters. Rejections become feedback the system learns from.

  5. 05 Hand it off

    You keep the workflow, the rules, and the docs to run it without me.

This is how I work, not a purchasable package.
