Cloud Consciousness / C Project 01

A voice mirror for memory, context, decisions, and self-dialogue.

The Brain vault is v0 of Cloud Consciousness: a private second-self interface that can hold Chay's patterns, interventions, projects, questions, decisions, and operating history.

What It Does

It should interrupt the right patterns, not just answer questions.

When Chay is spiraling, it asks whether the thinking is producing options or only verdicts.

When Chay is over-architecting, it asks what concrete artifact can move this forward.

When Chay is in hype mode, it captures the energy and converts it into one small commitment.

Private v0 Voice interface draft

Chay: My head is not straight. What am I missing here?

Cloud Consciousness: Are you analyzing or spiraling? Real thinking produces options. Give me the smallest action that changes the state of the problem in the next 30 minutes.
